Super Showcase

Super Showcase will be an interactive exploration of past work with levels similar to Super Mario where the user will navigate the character through a map layout and access different sections. Jumping on yellow mystery can lead to different sections, such as projects I've worked on, by changing the content on the page with a countdown and then navigating back to the game. Specific game assets such as collecting coins in the air will pop up a progress bar for a few seconds with reference to how much of my portfolio the player has unlocked.

Controls:

Space bar to jump

N button for inverse colors

Click on yellow mystery boxes for redirection
